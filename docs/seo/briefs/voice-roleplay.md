## Draft Copy

### Hero

Voice roleplay lets you step into a scene and speak with AI characters as the story unfolds. Instead of only typing prompts, you can use voice to respond, ask questions, improvise dialogue, and guide the next turn of the roleplay.

NovelAI is built for players who want more than a static chatbot. Each session can feel like a playable story: you choose the setup, meet characters, react in the moment, and shape what happens next.

### What You Can Do

- Speak with AI characters in roleplay scenes.
- Continue the story through voice or text.
- Explore romance, fantasy, school life, adventure, mystery, and slice-of-life scenarios.
- Move between character conversation and interactive storytelling.
- Start from a recommended story or create your own setup.

### How It Works

1. Choose a roleplay scene or character.
2. Enter the story setup.
3. Speak or type your response.
4. Let the AI character respond in context.
5. Continue the scene and guide the direction of the story.

### Why Voice Roleplay Feels Different

Text roleplay is great for detail, but voice makes the scene feel faster and more immediate. You can react naturally, try different tones, and stay inside the moment without writing every line.

NovelAI combines voice interaction with interactive story structure, so the experience is closer to playing a narrative game than chatting with a blank assistant.

### Popular Scenarios

- Romantic character roleplay
- Fantasy adventure scenes
- Daily life and companion stories
- Mystery and investigation scenes
- School life roleplay
- Original character drama

### FAQ

#### What is voice roleplay?

Voice roleplay is an interactive experience where you speak with AI characters and continue a story through spoken or typed responses.

#### Is voice roleplay different from AI character chat?

Yes. Character chat focuses on conversation, while voice roleplay adds a more immersive way to participate in scenes, dialogue, and story choices.
