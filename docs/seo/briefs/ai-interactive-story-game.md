## Draft Copy

### Hero

An AI interactive story game lets you play inside a story instead of only reading it. You choose what to say, what to do next, and how your character reacts. The story continues around your decisions.

NovelAI combines interactive fiction, roleplay, and character-driven scenes so each session can become a different narrative path.

### What You Can Do

- Start from a story premise.
- Talk with characters inside the scene.
- Make choices that guide the next turn.
- Explore romance, fantasy, mystery, adventure, and daily-life stories.
- Continue through text roleplay and supported interactive features.

### Why It Is Different From a Story Generator

A story generator usually creates a fixed piece of text. An AI interactive story game keeps the session open. You can interrupt, ask, respond, choose a new direction, or build the world as you play.

### FAQ

#### What is an AI interactive story game?

It is a story experience where AI responds to your choices, dialogue, and roleplay actions as the scene continues.

#### Is this the same as AI character chat?

Not exactly. Character chat focuses on conversation, while an interactive story game adds scenes, goals, choices, and narrative progression.
